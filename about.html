Our Mission

Driving the Future of Home Energy

Green Source Energy LLC was founded with a singular vision: to make sustainable energy accessible, efficient, and aesthetically pleasing for single-family homeowners.

Under the leadership of Lee Hummel, we have established ourselves as a trusted name in the industry. We understand that every home is unique, and a "one-size-fits-all" approach simply doesn't work. That's why we specialize in custom rooftop and ground mount solutions that maximize your energy production.
